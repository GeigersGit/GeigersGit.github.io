About Me

Hey

Hey there! My name is Brian and I'm a software engineering graduate from California State University, Monterey Bay. My interest for computer science ignited in high school when I took off-campus Java and game design classes at a nearby vocational school. Since then, I developed a taste for creating all kinds of visual experiences through code, such as music visualizations, games, and mobile applications.

I delved into the field of virtual reality at CSUMB, and have worked on multiple projects for the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and the Samsung GearVR headsets. I believe that the best technology experiences are the most immersive, so I have focused my studies on technologies that improve that aspect. I would ultimately love to work in the games industry in virtually any discipline, but I would be equally excited working on anything that combines code with visual design. If you’d like, send questions or just say hi at www.briangeiger@gmail.com. Thanks for stopping by!
