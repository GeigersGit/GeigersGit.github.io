About Me

Hey

Hey there! My name is Brian and I'm a software engineering graduate from California State University, Monterey Bay. My interest for computer science ignited in high school when I took off-campus Java and game design classes at a nearby vocational school. Since then, I developed a taste for creating all kinds of visual experiences through code, such as music visualizations, games, and mobile apps.

I delved into the field of virtual reality at CSUMB, and have worked on multiple projects for the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and the Samsung GearVR headsets. I think the best technology experiences are the most immersive, so I've focused my studies on improving that aspect. I would ultimately love to work in the games industry in virtually any discipline, but I would be equally excited working on anything that combines code with visual design.
